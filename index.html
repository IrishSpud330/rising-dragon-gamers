Welcome to Rising Dragon (RD) Gamers, we are a gaming group based in Bathurst and enjoy anything related to games. We currently meet on a weekly basis and boast a group of round 20 people. We provide a variety of games these consist of Role Plating Games, card games, VR games and Video games.
